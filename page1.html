About Us

Established in the year 2012, at New Delhi (India), we, “Air Comfort & Automation Company" are Authorized Business Partner of M/s Blue Star Ltd. We are renowned as one of the distinguished and fast growing refrigeration company for a comprehensive range of Cold Storage and Refrigeration Solutions, Banana Ripening Chambers, Mango Ripening Chambers, Cold Rooms, Freezer Rooms, Blast Freezers, Water Chillers, Commercial Air Conditioning, and Building Security Systems. We are the experts in customized designs, leading-edge manufacturing, and superior installation quality and prompt services. The hallmarks of our company are customer orientation, transparency and timely execution of projects. Apart from the above activities, we also use and promote best-in-class PUF Panels and also latest technology equipment manufactured by world renowned leaders such as Danfoss, Bitzer, Trane USA, Emerson, Von Amerongen, Europe, Thermal Master Korea. We are the authorized dealers for Blue Star range of Cold Storages, Banana Ripening Chambers and Commercial Refrigeration Systems e.g. Water Coolers, Deep Freezers, Display Cabinets etc. A large Team of talented and dedicated professionals of our company successfully implement every project since we fully understand the dynamics and intricacies of an application and the special requirements of our valued customers, to deliver powerful customized and satisfying solutions. Positive client centric approach and our efforts are directed towards providing optimum quality range of products. Our strong distribution network enables us to deliver our products to clients in assured time frame. We take care of clients’ budget and offer the products at very affordable prices. Apart from this, we are well-known among clients for our transparent dealings, ethical business policies, and easy modes of payment, wide distribution strength and fulfillment of delivery timelines.
