Cold Chain Solutions, Cold Storage and Logistics

Cold Chain is an unbrokentemperature-controlledsupply chain. It is used to preserve and to extendshelf lifeof products, such as fresh fruits & vegetables,seafood,processed food,frozen food, dairy products, ice cream,photographic film, chemicals andpharmaceutical and vaccines. Cold chain goods are heat-sensitive and necessitate all of the means used to ensure a constant temperature for a product that is perishable, from the time it is manufactured until the time it is consumed. Aeron Cold Chain Solutions is a fast growing organisation providing Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) based PUF insulated panels structures widely used in cold storages, food processing, warehousing, pack-houses, collection centres, dairy, meat processing plants etc. We undertake turnkey projects with integration of leading technologies and services to meet various climatic conditions. We also provide radically new energy management technology and solutions that reduce fossil fuels use thereby reducing cabon emissions. Our best-in-class cold chain solutions also come with cold chain monitoring system. A good near real-time temperature monitoring and analysis mechanism ensure cold chain reliablity.
